This fresh basil pesto is an easy, homemade sauce that can be used as a condiment or dressing for pasta, sandwiches, or vegetables.

**Cook time:** 5 minutes

**Yield:** 5 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 cup basil
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil

## Directions

1. In a food processor, add basil, garlic, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
2. Pulse food processor until smooth, slowly adding olive oil while pulsing.
3. Store in a sealed container and refrigerate.
