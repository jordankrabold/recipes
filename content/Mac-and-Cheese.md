Nice, simple, rich mac and cheese.

**Cook time:** 50 minutes

**Yield:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 8 oz. uncooked elbow macaroni
- 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 3 cups milk
- 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 1/2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 1 pinch paprika

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Cook macaroni to package directions. Drain.
3. In a saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce is a little thick.
4. Put macaroni in a large casserole dish. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir well.
5. Melt 2 tablespoon butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and brown.
6. Spread over macaroni and cheese to cover. Sprink paprika over top.
7. Bake for 30 minutes.
